Welcome To Freya

Freya is an emulator for the Ragnarök Online™ Gaming Protocol. It means that it will emulate the behaviour of a normal Ragnarök Online™ server, allowing you to experience the administration of a gaming server.

Anonymous Freya SVN access is available at http://svn.ff.st/freya/freya. We do not support it though.

The l33t Dev Team

Freya is Yors branch-off of eAthena, which is based on the original, by the Japanese Development Team.
